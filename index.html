How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same image touch, they merge into one!

In order to solve the game please follow the below image sequence. Please consider that the hierarchy within each company division (SPMs, Operations, MDs) has been randomly decided.

Senior PMs: Nikita (2), Claudia (4), Pranshu (8), Kaspar (16)

Operations: Felipe (32), Aiste (64), Sebastian (128)

Managing Directors: Marta (256), Carlos (512), Ammad (1024)

CEO: Mathias (2048)

PS: Please do not get desperate if you do not manage to solve the game. Statistics in other similar game, assuming the creation of a 2048 tile, show that just 1% of total games ended in a win, and just 5%-10% of total players have been able to solve it.

Math and Logic behind the game: Considering every tile image is a power of 2 (Nikita = 2; Claudia = 4; Pranshu = 8 etc), a player to win the game (Win = Mathias Wengeler = 2048) would ideally need to make 2^{11-1}-1=1023 matches. A single tile needs to be doubled ten times to get from Nikita (2) to Mathias (2048) (nine times if starting from a four (Claudia)).Atheneum
